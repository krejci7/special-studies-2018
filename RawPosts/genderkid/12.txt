Activism, Politics and Mass Media Celebration Legal gender gender identity law news pictures 
Argentina’s gender identity law went live this week, which means –among other things– that we can now easily change our name and legal gender. So exciting! On Monday, Facebook exploded with pictures and updates from happy friends who had gone to start their paperwork.
