Metafilter featured this interesting blog sponsored by the Sydney Morning Herald (and apparently named after this Nick Lowe song). I was also impressed with how sensitive he was to trans people.  Also, he hooks up with a woman while cross-dressed, which (although not unheard of), is pretty cool.
