From Huffington Post: ABC’s hit TV series “Modern Family” will feature TV’s first transgender child in a groundbreaking show on Wednesday. Shows such as “Orange is the New Black” and “Nashville” have cast adult transgender actors to play transgender characters. But “Modern Family” newcomer Jackson Millarker, who openly identifies as transgender, is the first child actor to appear in such a role. More here…
