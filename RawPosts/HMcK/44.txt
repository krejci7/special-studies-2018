The MN T-Girls will be taking a road trip this month to Saint Cloud’s Pride weekend!  There are events all throughout the weekend, from theater shows on Friday, Saturday’s Pride in the Park and drag show, to a Sunday brunch.  The T-Girls and I will be at Pride in the Park.  I hope to start attending more of Minnesota’s pride festivals and I am excited for this! If you plan on going, drop me an email!  I’d love to meet you! Love, Hannah
