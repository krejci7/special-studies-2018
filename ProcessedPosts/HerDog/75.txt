I joined a Facebook group for trans guys over 40 moderated by one of the panelists. The workshops also discussed why you need\u00a0social networks and peer support. The highlight of my trip was running into other bloggers and readers. I met\u00a0Kameron (janitorqueer) and their partner, and I had \u00a0lunch with Shawn (Dawn to Don). I need to find a primary care physician regardless of whether I take hormones and I need to learn how to listen to my body. I need to stop apologizing for identifying as both butch and transgender.
