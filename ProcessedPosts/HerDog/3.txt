I want to reduce the frequency of events, but it won\u2019t happen unless I make it happen. In 2016, I decided to stop using the women\u2019s locker room at the gym and to use they/them/their pronouns in my political work. I never thought I would feel good about my body, and I need to learn how to acknowledge it and hold onto it. 2017 for me is not about passing or being read as male. In 2017, I want to stop apologizing for not being on testosterone, and to stop apologizing for thinking about starting it. I want to stop expecting, or asking, other people to validate my transness.
