It\u2019s going to be stunning. Technicalities: every day, I apply 5 grams of 1% testosterone gel (that makes 50 milligrams of T). It\u2019s enough for many cis men to maintain their hormone levels if they don\u2019t produce enough/any T, and it seems to be an average starting dose for trans men, although many have it increased after a doctor checks their blood levels.
