We trans people are diverse, but we have a lot in common with each other and with lesbians, gay men and bisexuals. An alliance is not absolute trust, it\u2019s just a recognition of shared goals.  When you\u2019re dealing with goals that aren\u2019t shared, the alliance is meaningless. We trans people who are not most at risk should participate too, out of solidarity and because every once in a while one of us gets killed too.  Our dependable allies should be there too.  But on this day, and on this day most of all, we should reach out beyond our normal alliances and build new alliances around the shared principle of not killing us.
