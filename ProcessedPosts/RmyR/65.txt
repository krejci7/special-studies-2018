When we first discovered that C.J. was gender nonconforming, we\u2019d hide all of the \u201cgirl stuff\u201d in our house when Chase had a friend over. C.J. promptly ran upstairs and threw on the white, faux-minx robe that he inherited from Nana \u2014 which he likes to put on at night after his warm bath and after applying his leave-in conditioner and throwing his hair up in a French twist. Lucky for me (and Chase) I had just purchased Disney\u2019s Inside Out as a surprise for C.J. to watch while we snuggled and ate popcorn in my bed upstairs. After the movie, Chase and his friends retreated to his room to play video games for a bit. Lots of yelling, banging around and falling ensued behind Chase\u2019s closed door. Apparently that\u2019s how middle school boys play video games. C.J. kept sneaking to Chase\u2019s door and I caught him writing notes on his favorite fluorescent pink notepad and pushing them under the door. I could only imagine what he was writing.
