However, the concept of being transgender never confused or fascinated me. It just seemed so simple, pursuing alignment to match one\u2019s gender identity. I am so grateful to all the people and clients I interacted with during that time; I learned so much from them and became more committed to and invested in my work than ever. At first my gender therapy was only with adult clients. Soon, my name was \u201cout there\u201d and I began seeing gender nonconforming and transgender children. Since my specialty had always been working with children and my newfound specialty was gender therapy, this was a beautiful and serendipitous melding of the two.
