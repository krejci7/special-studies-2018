For those of you not in the field of mental health or well-trained in interviewing children, here are a few tips for completing this worksheet:  Don\u2019t make a big deal of the sheet. Look at the worksheet. Poise your pen or pencil over the blanks and begin asking the questions. Write the answer in the blank as soon as they are stated, without a reaction (facial expression, question, anything!) Children are incredibly in tune with others\u2019 responses to what they are saying, particularly if the \u201cother\u201d is a parent. When the worksheet is completed, go back and ask questions for clarification.
